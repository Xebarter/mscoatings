Upgrade the existing Ms Coatings admin dashboard into a lightweight ERP system with an integrated Point of Sale (POS) system. The goal is to create a complete business management platform for a car paint wholesale and retail business.

The system should retain all existing features (product management and online payment handling) while adding the following MVP ERP and POS capabilities:

1. POS (Point of Sale) System
- Create a fast and user-friendly sales interface for counter sales.
- Support barcode scanning using:
  - USB barcode scanners
  - Mobile phone camera barcode scanning (where possible)
- Allow searching products by:
  - Barcode
  - Product name
  - Category
  - Brand
  - Paint code/model reference
- Automatically fetch product details when scanned:
  - Product name
  - Category
  - Selling price
  - Available stock
- Add products to a shopping cart.
- Allow quantity adjustments.
- Apply discounts (with permission controls).
- Support multiple payment methods:
  - Cash
  - Mobile Money
  - Bank transfer
  - Card payments
  - Online payments already integrated
- Generate and print receipts.
- Store every sale as a transaction record.
- Allow sales returns and cancellations with proper authorization.

2. Inventory Management
- Convert current product management into a complete inventory system.
- Track:
  - Current stock levels
  - Stock movement history
  - Incoming stock
  - Sold stock
  - Damaged/lost stock adjustments
- Automatically reduce inventory after every sale.
- Provide low-stock alerts.
- Allow manual stock adjustments.
- Support product variations such as:
  - Paint brand
  - Paint type
  - Colour/code
  - Size/volume
  - Packaging unit

3. Purchase and Supplier Management
- Add supplier management.
- Record purchases from suppliers.
- Create purchase orders.
- Update inventory automatically after receiving stock.
- Track supplier payment status.

4. Customer Management (CRM)
- Create customer profiles.
- Store:
  - Customer name
  - Phone number
  - Purchase history
  - Total spending
  - Outstanding balances
- Support customer accounts for repeat buyers.
- Add loyalty points or customer discounts (MVP-ready structure).

5. Sales and Financial Reporting
Create dashboards showing:
- Daily, weekly, monthly sales
- Best-selling products
- Revenue trends
- Profit estimates
- Stock valuation
- Payment method breakdown
- Sales by employee/user

6. Employee and User Management
Add role-based permissions:
- Admin
- Manager
- Sales staff
- Inventory staff

Control access to:
- Viewing reports
- Changing prices
- Applying discounts
- Adjusting stock
- Processing refunds

7. Expense Tracking (Basic MVP)
Add simple expense recording:
- Expense category
- Amount
- Date
- Description
- Person responsible

8. Audit Trail
Track important actions:
- Product updates
- Price changes
- Stock adjustments
- Sales cancellations
- Refunds
- User activity

9. Dashboard Improvements
Create an ERP overview dashboard with:
- Today's sales
- Today's profit estimate
- Low-stock products
- Recent transactions
- Pending supplier payments
- Customer balances

10. System Requirements
- Keep the interface modern, fast, and responsive.
- Optimize the POS screen for tablets and desktop computers.
- Ensure barcode scanning works smoothly during high-volume sales.
- Maintain compatibility with the existing database structure where possible.
- Do not remove existing online payment functionality.
- Build this as an MVP first, focusing on reliability and ease of use rather than unnecessary complexity.

The final result should be a complete retail ERP/POS solution suitable for managing a car paint wholesale and retail store.
